HISTORY OF CANDY

The word candy comes from the Persian word for "cane sugar",(نیشکر), and probably also from Sanskrit khanda, which means "piece (of sugar).Any of a variety of confections made with sugar, syrup, etc., often combined with chocolate, fruit, nuts, etc."

MY FAVORITE CANDIES

Milk Duds

A chewy coconut milk caramel center is covered in 60% dark chocolate that has been tossed with organic crisped quinoa for an irresistibly snackable treat.Milk Duds are a caramel candy with vegetable oil and cocoa coating. It was owned by Hershey Company in 1926. In 2008, Hershey Company changed the ingredients of its product to retrieve the price of the candy and also changed the packaging and description of the product along with the ingredients.

Jolly Rancher

The Jolly Rancher is a hard candy of sour/sweet or tangy taste. It’s an American brand of Hershey Company. It was introduced on 22 November, 1949. This candy has many flavors like watermelon, apple, blue raspberry and sriracha, cherry and habanero, ginger, grape, and apple.
